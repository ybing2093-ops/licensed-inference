# Disclaimer

This repository implements **endpoint sufficiency diagnostics and inference-time gating** under an explicit evaluation protocol.

It does **not** claim:
- consciousness, self-awareness, personhood, or subjective experience
- a universal notion of "truth" independent of declared domain gates and verifiers

The outputs `LICENSED / NOT_LICENSED / UNDEFINED` are **protocol-level** statuses:
they indicate whether the system treats endpoint information as sufficient at reporting resolution, and whether escalation or abstention is recommended.
